Welcome

Hello, I'm Felipe Sanchez, a Research Fellow in the Mortality & Health Inequalities Group at the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine (LSHTM). My research focuses on forecasting mortality and fertility rates using hierarchical Bayesian modeling, particularly in contexts with fragmented or incomplete data. I investigate how education, as a structural determinant, influences demographic and health outcomes. These insights inform evidence-based policymaking in public health, labor markets, and social development.
