Welcome

Hello, I’m Felipe Sanchez, a postgraduate researcher at The University of Manchester, specializing in forecasting mortality and fertility rates using hierarchical Bayesian modeling. My research focuses on analyzing fragmented data to develop more accurate forecasts by incorporating education as a critical variable. I hypothesize that education plays a significant role in influencing both mortality and fertility rates, with implications that extend beyond demographics to affect labor markets and other socio-economic factors. My work aims to provide more nuanced insights that can inform policy-making and strategic planning in areas related to public health, education, and economic development.
