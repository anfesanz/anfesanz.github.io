Welcome

Hello, I'm Felipe Sanchez, a postgraduate researcher at The University of Manchester. My research specializes in forecasting mortality and fertility rates using hierarchical Bayesian modeling. I focus on analyzing fragmented data to develop more accurate forecasts by incorporating education as a critical variable. I hypothesize that education plays a significant role in influencing both mortality and fertility rates. The implications of this extend beyond demographics, affecting labor markets and other socio-economic factors. My work aims to provide more nuanced insights that can inform policy-making and strategic planning in public health, education, and economic development.
