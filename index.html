Welcome

Hello, I'm Felipe Sanchez. I am a postgraduate researcher (PGR) at The University of Manchester. My research focuses on forecasting mortality and fertility rates using hierarchical Bayesian modeling to analyze fragmented data. The core hypothesis of my thesis is that education must be included in these forecasts, as it significantly influences both mortality and fertility rates. Education’s impact extends beyond demographics, affecting the labor market and various other factors that indirectly shape these rates. Explore my research to discover how these dynamics unfold and their broader implications.
